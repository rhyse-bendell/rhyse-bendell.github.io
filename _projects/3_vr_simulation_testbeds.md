This project theme develops custom Unreal Engine and VR-based simulation environments for controlled experimentation in human-agent and human-robot teaming contexts. Testbeds are built to provide repeatable scenarios while preserving ecological realism for operationally relevant behavior.

The environments support experimental manipulations of interface design, teammate behavior, and task complexity so that team dynamics, decision quality, and performance can be assessed under comparable conditions.
