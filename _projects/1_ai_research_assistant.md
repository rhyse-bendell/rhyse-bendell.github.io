This project theme focuses on building and evaluating LLM-enabled workflows that support literature synthesis, evidence extraction, and structured research execution. The workflow design keeps humans in the loop for quality control, interpretation, and validation while using automation for repetitive synthesis and organization tasks.

Representative outputs include assistant-style pipelines for drafting research summaries, organizing source material, and streamlining early-stage analysis for human-subjects research programs.
