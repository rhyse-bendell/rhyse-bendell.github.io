## Selected Work

### Human-AI interaction and collaborative performance under uncertainty (2024)
This paper examines team outcomes when human and AI agents coordinate under uncertainty, with a focus on measurable performance impacts in operational settings.

### Trust, transparency, and human-robot teaming performance in complex tasks (2023)
This work analyzes how interface transparency and trust calibration influence human-robot teaming quality and objective task outcomes.

### Designing and evaluating human-AI teaming systems for operational performance (2025)
My dissertation presents an integrated framework for designing and evaluating human-AI teaming systems through controlled experiments and simulation-based studies.
